TInteger TTCAPDialogue::getcurprimitiveid()

To get the currently received primitive ID

no arguments

1: tc-begin
2: tc-continue1
3: tc-continue2

5: tc-u-abort
6: tc-p-abort
9: tc-notice
16: tc-invoke
17: tc-result-nl
18: tc-result-l
19: tc-u-error
20: tc-u-reject
21: tc-l-cancel
22: tc-u-cancel
23: tc-l-reject
24: tc-r-reject
